# Vehicle Parking System with built-in garage, impound, job garages, and mileage tracking

## Features

- ESX framework support.
- Built-in mileage tracking and display.
- Transfer vehicle ownership to players.
- Tracks vehicle damage and can save Config.SaveVehicleDamages
- Vehicle personal spot system.
- Optional ox_target support for quick impound actions.
- Impound system with:
  - Police/job-restricted impound access
  - Impound reason, fee, and impound staff tracking
  
## Commands

- `/vbuy` - Buy a parking spot.
- `/vlist` - Open a list of your owned vehicles.
- `/vpark` - Park your vehicle at your owned parking spot.
- `/vfix` - Repair your current vehicle.
